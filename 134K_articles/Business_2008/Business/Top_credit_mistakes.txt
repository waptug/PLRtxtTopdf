Title: 
Top credit mistakes

Word Count:
240

Summary:
Today, I will be posting some of the most common credit card mistakes, people make. These 
mistakes may go un-noticed or often ignored, but they do leave marks on 
your credit report.


Keywords:
credit repair,credit mistakes


Article Body:
<ul>
  <li>Using expensive or undesirable types of credit costs too much and is 
  negatively scored</li>
  <li>Accumulating too many lines of credit or too many credit cards causes 
  credit report remarks like &quot;too much consumer credit.&quot;</li>
  <li>3. Only paying the minimum due keeps balances too high. make sure you make 
  it a habit to always pay more than minimum</li>
  <li>4. Maxing out your credit card will drop your score dramatically</li>
  <li>5. Cash advances from Credit cards cost higher interest rates and credit 
  cards companies love you, when you do that</li>
  <li>6. Exceeding limits and having to pay over-limit fees is a negative with 
  creditors and causes &quot;high proportional amounts owed&quot; remarks on credit 
  reports and subtracts credit score points.</li>
  <li>&nbsp;Charging more than you can afford causes a snowball effect of debt 
  with no easy way to pay it off.</li>
  <li>Ignoring credit problems causes unnecessary negative impact. Talk to your 
  creditors, and give them a heads up that you are about to be late. This wont 
  always work, but sometimes creditors wont report it to bureaus</li>
  <li>Always report change of address to your creditors in a timely manner</li>
</ul>
