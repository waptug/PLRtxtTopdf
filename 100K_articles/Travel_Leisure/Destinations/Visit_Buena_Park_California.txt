<B>Buena Park</B>, California, just under seven (7) miles down the freeway from <B>Disneyland</B> in <a href="http://anaheim.usacitydirectories.com" target="noframes">Anaheim</a>, is a city of 80,000 plus people and is a tourist attraction city that is overlooked by many tourists. This city is home to <B>Knotts Berry Farm</B>, "With over 165 wild rides, shows and unique attractions just steps from Knott's Resort Hotel, Knott's Theme Park is the place to go for family fun and thrill seeking adventure!", <B>Knotts Soak City</B>, "Knott's Soak City Water Parks are acres of waterlogged fun for everyone.",  <B>Medieval Times Dinner & Tournament</B>, "A place where knights battle for their kingdom's honor while an exceptional feast is served in a magnificent setting.", <B>Movieland Wax Museum</B>, "Movieland Wax Museum is the most unique House of Wax and one of the world's largest wax museums." and <B>Ripley’s Believe It Or Not</B>, "The museum houses hundreds of rare and unusual artifacts, some which are unavailable anywhere else in the world.". All of these attractions are within walking distance of each other.<br><br>
The city hosts a fair variety of inexpensive but good hotels including, Courtyard By Marriott, Embassy Suites, Holiday Inn, Radisson, Red Roof Inn and more and a good selection of restaurants including, Claim Jumper, Knott’s Chicken Dinner Restaurant, Hometown Buffet, Outbacks Steakhouse, Pat & Oscars, Ventanas and more.<br><br>
Do you want something else to do, then try sportfishing, sailing, harbor cruises or whale watching in nearby <a href="http://newportbeach.usacitydirectories.com" target="noframes">Newport Beach</a>, try shopping at the Fashion Island in Newport Beach or the South Coast Plaza in <a href="http://costamesa.usacitydirectories.com" target=noframes">Costa Mesa</a>, visit the Aquarium Of The Pacific (one of the largest aquariums in the United States.) in close by <a href="http://longbeach.usacitydirectories.com" target=noframes">Long Beach</a>, spend a day at the beach in beautiful <a href="http://lagunabeach.usacitydirectories.com" target=noframes">Laguna Beach</a> or take a twenty two (22) mile drive up the freeway to <a href="http://losangeles.usacitydirectories.com" target=noframes">Los Angeles</a>.<br><br>
